Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GrinSOIL collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: February 19, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Username and password
  • Email address
  • Role (Farmer or Customer)
  • Optional: profile picture, phone number

Transaction Information

  • Purchase history
  • Shipping information
  • Product listings (for Farmers)
  • Payment details (processed securely through third-party providers)

Automatic Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system, IP address)
  • Browser information
  • Usage data (pages visited, actions taken)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process transactions between Farmers and Customers
  • Send notifications about expiring products, new listings, and orders
  • Verify account information
  • Personalize your experience
  • Ensure the security of our platform
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

Other Users

When you use our platform, certain information is shared between Farmers and Customers to facilitate transactions:

  • Farmers: Your username, contact information, and product details are shared with Customers who purchase your products
  • Customers: Your username, shipping address, and order details are shared with Farmers whose products you purchase

Service Providers

We work with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Payment processors
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Email service providers
  • Analytics services

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety or property
  • Respond to legal requests from public authorities

Note: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure user authentication
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls for our systems

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Object to certain data processing activities

You can exercise these rights by:

  • Adjusting your account settings
  • Contacting us directly using the information below

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Improve our services

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete that information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
